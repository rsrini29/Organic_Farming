FARMING STORY YOU WILL NEVER FORGET!

Go Farm is a new business idea to strengthen the connection between rural and urban people, grow farm visits, and provide a new agri-tourism income stream for farmers.

Today, a visitor to a rural area has no easy way to visit a farm unless they have a personal connection to the farmer. Go Farm ,online platform gives consumers the opportunity to book unique agricultural experiences directly with farmers in areas they visit.
